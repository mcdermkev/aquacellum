# POSEIDON + ECHO — Very Light v1 (One Page Summary)

**Goal for First Beta**  
Ship a small, optional, early version of Poseidon (AI) + Echo (the fish) so users can talk normally and see their fish react. Keep it very light and safe.

### What We’re Shipping (Light v1)
- Basic conversational onboarding (user describes tank in plain words)
- Simple husbandry logging by typing (“I fed the fish”, “I did a water test”)
- Echo reacts visually (swims faster, small glows, looks happy)
- Clearly labeled as “Early / Experimental”
- Easy to turn on or ignore

### What We’re NOT Shipping
- No complex fish advice
- No camera or photos
- No voice input
- No automatic actions
- No breeding or buying recommendations

### How It Feels
User talks to Poseidon → Poseidon does something small and helpful → Echo (the fish) reacts in the tank.  
This creates a simple, nice feeling that the app has a little life.

### Where It Lives
- Inside the normal tank view
- Simple chat box the user can open when they want
- Works in both Casual and Pro mode

### Why We’re Doing This Light Version
- Gets something magical into the beta early
- Low risk of slowing down launch
- Lets us learn what users actually like
- Easy to improve later based on real feedback

### Success Look Like
People try it, smile when Echo reacts, and it doesn’t confuse or frustrate them.

**Status:** Planning only — very small scope for first beta.
