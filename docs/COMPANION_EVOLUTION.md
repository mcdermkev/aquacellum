# Understanding Your Breeder Companion’s Evolution

### What's the purpose of the Breeder Companion?
The Breeder Companion is an interactive, visual representation of your husbandry discipline and dedication to the aquaculture community. It acts as a living metric of your performance as an aquarist, reflecting the health and activity of your fish room, your genetic documentation rigor, and your engagement in mentoring other hobbyists. It rewards daily care practices with a tangible, visual companion that swims inside your logbook, bridging real-world husbandry with decentralized achievements.

---

## 1. Lifecycle Stages vs. Progression Tiers

To understand your companion's development, it is important to distinguish between **Lifecycle Stages** and **Progression Tiers**:
- **Lifecycle Stages** represent the physical form and state of your companion (such as being a quiet mystery egg or hatching into an active swimming entity).
- **Progression Tiers** represent your achievement rankings and experience level (ranging from Bronze up to the ultimate God-Tier).

---

## 2. The Companion Lifecycle Stages

Your companion progresses through distinct physical forms as you earn Experience Points (XP):

```
  +--------------------------------+                  +-----------------------------------+
  |   🥚 Stage 1: Quiet Mystery    |   ──(1,500 XP)──>  |   🐟 Stage 2: Hatched Companion   |
  |   (Egg unlocked at 500 XP)     |                  |   (Swims dynamically in dashboard)|
  +--------------------------------+                  +-----------------------------------+
```

- **Stage 1: Quiet Mystery Egg (0 - 1,499 XP)**: Unlocked automatically when your profile reaches **500 XP**. Your companion appears as a small, translucent, interactive egg resting quietly in the bottom right corner of your biotope banner. Tapping it prematurely displays the message: *"Wait, it's not Easter?! 🥚"*.
- **Stage 2: Hatched Companion (1,500+ XP)**: Once you reach **1,500 XP**, the egg hatches! A floating, autonomous 2D vector fish emerges and begins swimming around your biotope tank panel, bouncing smoothly off the container boundaries.

---

## 3. Progression Tiers

As your companion gathers more XP, it evolves through several achievement rankings, altering its size, color filters, and glow animations:

| Tier | XP Threshold | Visual Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bronze** | 1,500 XP | A tiny, translucent fry (scale 0.8) with simple swimming mechanics. |
| **Silver** | 2,500 XP | A larger juvenile fish (scale 1.1) with a sleek silver/blue saturation filter and a subtle drop shadow. |
| **Gold** | 5,000 XP | An adult breeder (scale 1.4) wrapped in a golden drop-shadow aura glow. |
| **Master** | 10,000 XP | A majestic specimen (scale 1.7) displaying a deep neon purple drop-shadow aura. |
| **God-Tier** | 10,000+ XP + #1 Rank | A legendary golden Koi model (scale 2.2) with custom flowy dorsal and trailing tail fins, driving a flashing neon pulse animation.<br>*Note: Only one God-Tier Champion can exist per region at any time.* |

---

## 4. Regional God-Tier Usurpation

### Only one God-Tier Champion can exist per region at any time.
If you have reached Master Tier (10,000+ XP) and your XP becomes strictly the highest in your geographical region (defined by the fuzzed `zoneHash`), your companion will mutate into the **God-Tier Champion**. If another breeder in your zone earns more XP than you, they will usurp the crown. Your companion will be demoted back to **Master** tier, encouraging ongoing healthy competition and husbandry excellence.

---

## 5. Experience Points (XP) Category Comparison

All activities in the Aquadex system generate one of two types of experience: **Hobbyist XP** or **Prestige XP**. Use the table below to see how points are categorized and earned:

| XP Type | Purpose / Focus | How it is Earned | Point Breakdown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hobbyist XP** | Daily care, maintenance, and catalog browsing | - Daily feeding log<br>- Water chemistry snapshot<br>- Marketplace checkout actions | - Feeding: `+10 XP`<br>- Water Test: `+15 XP` |
| **Prestige XP** | Advanced breeding, professional audits, and community mentorship | - Specimen registration<br>- Documenting Sire/Dam lineage<br>- Performing or receiving expert audits | - Registration: `+20 XP`<br>- Expert Audit (Commenter): `+25 XP`<br>- Receiving Audit (Owner): `+50 XP` |

### Expo Events (Double XP)
Registering transactions inside an active GPS Expo Zone awards **2x XP** on all transaction handshakes, dispatching the notification:
> `⚡ POKÉMON GO-STYLE LIVE EVENT DOUBLE XP UNLOCKED!`
