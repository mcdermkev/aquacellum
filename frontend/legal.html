Privacy Policy & Data Collection

Effective Date: June 6, 2026 · Last Updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Aquacellum collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Display name and bio (chosen during onboarding)
  • Email address (if provided for waitlist or account recovery)
  • Profile avatar photos
  • Tank data, water parameters, action logs, and specimen records
  • Posts, comments, species insights, and photos shared on The Reef
  • Marketplace listings and transaction details

Information collected automatically:

  • Wallet address (via Privy embedded wallet or MetaMask connection)
  • Device type and browser information (for responsive rendering)
  • Approximate geographic location (for Tides event proximity and marketplace radar — fuzzed to a 1–3 mile radius, never exact)
  • Push notification subscription tokens (if you opt in to Web Push)

Information we do NOT collect:

  • We do not run any analytics or tracking scripts (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Vercel Analytics).
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not collect your exact home or facility address.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Platform operation: To display your profile, render your tanks and species data, process marketplace transactions, and deliver notifications.
  • AI features: Your tank data and text inputs may be processed by Gemini AI (via Google Cloud) to provide Poseidon responses, alt-text generation, and species suggestions. We do not send your personal identity to AI services — only operational data like species names, water parameters, and your natural-language queries.
  • Social features: To display your posts, comments, and profile on The Reef to other users based on your visibility settings.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

3. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

Your data is stored across the following services:

  • Local device (Dexie.js / localStorage): Tank data, action logs, species cache, companion state. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly publish to the blockchain or post to The Reef.
  • Supabase (PostgreSQL): Social layer data — profiles, posts, comments, reactions, notifications, media URLs. Hosted in the US. Protected by Row-Level Security policies.
  • Supabase Storage: Uploaded photos (profile avatars, tank photos, Reef post images). Delivered via CDN.
  • Base Sepolia blockchain: On-chain specimen registrations, species catalog entries, and marketplace transactions. Blockchain data is public and immutable.
  • Privy: Wallet creation and authentication session management. See Privy's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Frontend hosting and serverless API functions. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud (Gemini AI): AI query processing for Poseidon, search parsing, and alt-text generation. No personal data is sent — only operational queries.

4. Local Storage & Browser Data

Aquacellum uses browser localStorage and IndexedDB (via Dexie.js) to store your operational data locally. This includes:

  • Your tank configurations, action logs, and species data
  • Companion (Echo) state and XP progress
  • Cached species manifest for offline use
  • Draft social posts and notification state

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you take an explicit action (like publishing a specimen on-chain or posting to The Reef). You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings or the in-app data export/delete tools.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Local data: Persists until you clear your browser data or use the in-app export/delete function.
  • Supabase data: Retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your profile, posts, and social data by emailing kevin@aquacellum.com.
  • Blockchain data: On-chain records (specimen registrations, transactions) are immutable and cannot be deleted. This is a fundamental property of blockchain technology.
  • Beta wipe: All off-chain data (profiles, social posts, local caches) may be wiped before mainnet launch. On-chain testnet data will be abandoned when we migrate to Base mainnet.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Aquacellum is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it. If you believe a child under 13 is using the platform, please contact us at kevin@aquacellum.com.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your off-chain data.
  • Export your data in a portable format (JSON export is available in-app).
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via your browser or in-app settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email kevin@aquacellum.com.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) on all Supabase database tables.
  • JWT-based authentication bridging Privy wallet sessions to Supabase.
  • Server-side API keys for AI services (never exposed to the client).
  • Location fuzzing (1–3 mile radius) to protect facility addresses.
  • Commit-reveal cryptographic schemes for in-person transaction verification.

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data but will notify affected users promptly in the event of a breach.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via the platform. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: kevin@aquacellum.com

Community Guidelines

Effective Date: June 6, 2026 · Last Updated: June 6, 2026

The Reef is Aquacellum's social layer — a space for sharing tank updates, connecting with fellow fishkeepers, and building trust through genuine aquaculture activity. These guidelines exist to keep that space healthy.

1. Be Respectful

  • Treat every community member with basic courtesy, regardless of experience level.
  • No harassment, personal attacks, hate speech, discrimination, or threats of any kind.
  • Disagreements about care practices are welcome — frame them as learning opportunities, not attacks.
  • Don't spam, flood the feed, or use the platform for self-promotion unrelated to aquaculture.

2. Keep It Honest

  • No fraudulent listings: Do not create marketplace listings for specimens you don't own or species you've misidentified.
  • No falsified lineage: Pedigree records must accurately reflect actual breeding pairs. Fabricating ancestry is a bannable offense.
  • No misleading photos: Photos should accurately represent the specimen or tank being discussed. Heavy color grading, filters that misrepresent species coloration, or using others' photos without credit is prohibited.
  • Species Insights accuracy: Tips and breeding notes should be based on personal experience or established sources. Don't post unverified medical advice.

3. Content Standards

  • All content must be related to aquaculture, fishkeeping, or the Aquacellum platform.
  • No explicit, violent, or disturbing content.
  • No illegal content or promotion of illegal activities (including illegal wildlife trade).
  • Respect intellectual property — don't post content you don't have rights to share.

4. Marketplace Conduct

  • List only specimens you currently own and can fulfil.
  • Accurately describe species, health conditions, and care requirements.
  • Honor committed transactions — backing out after a buyer locks escrow damages community trust.
  • Do not use the platform to facilitate trades of endangered species protected by CITES or local law.
  • Cash handshake bypass (Expo Mode) is intended for legitimate swap meets only. Abuse of this feature will result in suspension.

5. AI Interactions

  • Poseidon and Echo are AI assistants, not veterinarians. Do not rely on AI outputs for medical treatment decisions.
  • Do not attempt to manipulate AI responses for harmful purposes or to generate inappropriate content.
  • Report any AI outputs that seem harmful, inaccurate, or inappropriate.

6. Reporting & Enforcement

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

  • Email kevin@aquacellum.com with a description and, if possible, a screenshot or link.

Enforcement escalation:

  1. Warning: First offense or minor violations receive a direct warning.
  2. Temporary mute: Repeated violations or moderate offenses result in temporary restriction from posting on The Reef (24h–7 days).
  3. Suspension: Serious violations (fraud, harassment, repeated abuse) result in account suspension.
  4. Permanent ban: Egregious or repeat offenses result in permanent removal from the platform.

We reserve the right to skip escalation steps for severe violations. All enforcement decisions are final during beta.

7. Schools & Tides Events

  • School founders are responsible for maintaining a welcoming environment in their club channels.
  • Tides events (expos, virtual meetups, auctions) follow all standard community guidelines.
  • Auction bidding must be genuine — shill bidding or bid manipulation will result in immediate suspension.

8. Updates

These guidelines may be updated as the community grows. We'll announce material changes on The Reef. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the current guidelines.

💡 The short version: Be kind, be honest, keep it about fish, and help us build something we're all proud of.
